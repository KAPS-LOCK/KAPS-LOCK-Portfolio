KAPS LOCK™

We build things that shouldn't work — until they do.

KAPS LOCK™ is a small collective that meets in Discord calls and empty classrooms to build things over a weekend that probably shouldn't exist by Monday. Security tools, strange interfaces, things that talk to other things they shouldn't. No roadmap, no funding, just a shared appetite for shipping something real before the coffee runs out.
